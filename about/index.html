Hi, I’m Jay Gandhi 👋

I am a Polyglot Software Engineer based in Bengaluru, India, with 9 years of experience architecting and building high-performance platforms, developer ecosystems, and large-scale infrastructure. My career spans scaling tech stacks from pre-seed startups to optimizing core platforms for global enterprises like ServiceNow.

I specialize in Platform Engineering, Distributed Data Systems, and Front-end Infrastructure. I enjoy working where systems engineering meets front-end performance—building tooling, frameworks, and foundational platforms that increase system predictability and accelerate development velocity.

Currently, I am taking a self-directed career break to dive deep into AI systems, multi-agent orchestration, and core software engineering fundamentals.

#🛠️ What I Do Best

  • Platform & Core Infrastructure: Designing frameworks, composable systems, and infrastructure engines that power rapid product iteration while handling high throughput reliably.
  • Developer Experience (DevX) & Advocacy: Aligning cross-functional teams (Design, Product, Eng) to establish robust platform standards, build interactive tooling (GraphQL/REST explorers), and deploy automated testing solutions.
  • Observability & Performance Engineering: Building synthetic monitoring engines, telemetry SDKs, and data processing pipelines to minimize MTTD and optimize high-concurrency UI/UX runtimes.
  • Data Pipelines & Event-Driven Systems: Architecting distributed analytics architectures, scalable web scrapers, and streaming processing (Kafka/Flink, Spark, Athena/Presto) to clean, transform, and analyze multi-terabyte datasets.
